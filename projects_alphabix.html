ALPHABIX

Branding & Packaging

Alphabix has been created as a solution for promoting reading to young school children. As research progresses it is clear that many children find it difficult to learn in a traditional classroom environment. This product allows for a visual and interactive means of learning how to read, with the added benefit of the product being a delicious treat!

Alphabix includes New Zealand iconography with the Possum mascot and provides instructions for fun games and a suggested reading list all on the package.
