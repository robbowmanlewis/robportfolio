BACKYARD CITY

Photographic Publication

During an afternoon in Auckland City, I found myself camera in hand, taking photos of friends playing basketball. I had no intention of doing anything with the photos, however I warmed to the idea of producing a zine with a few works from the shoot for personal experience.
