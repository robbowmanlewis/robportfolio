FOLIO MAGAZINE

Editorial Photography & Magazine Cover Design

Folio is an imagined magazine involving cultural content such as sport, art and music. This issue contains an editorial of Cameron McLeod who plays the sport of inline hockey for New Zealand. The photographic process aimed at highlighting the emotional investment that one must put into sport in order to be the best.
