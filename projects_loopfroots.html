LOOP FROOTS

Animation

Lupe Froots is an animation project to interperate a serious quote in a humorous way. The quote used was by Thomas Edison; "I have not failed, I've just found 1000 ways that wont work."

My interpretation of this was to establish a narrative with a mad scientist having just created his 1000th failed experiement.
