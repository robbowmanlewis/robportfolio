PORTABLE STUDIO

Photography Series

I had a number of concerns with photographing in a studio. While studios can make for great photographs, I found a repetitiveness between the work done by different photographers.

To counter this restriction I came up with the idea of the Portable Studio which works by taking the subject, photographer and studio out into any environment. This worked by having an assistant holding a backing piece of white card, a subject, and me as the photographer. My intention with this was to allow the outside environment to subtly flood the photograph with new and interesting features that are otherwise unobtainable from a studio such as the effects of lighting and facial expressions.
