APPROACH

You may have noticed an icosahedron shape at the corner of this site. It's purpose is not restricted to taking you back to the home page. Yes it functions that way, however its true intention is to portray my approach to design. Throughout my growth as a designer, I have noticed other designers restrict themselves to a particular design style or a specific method of design production. While these people produce incredible work, I can't align myself with their process.

For me, design should not be limited to one face or one side, as represented by the icosahedron. Many sides make up this 3D form as I try to use many styles and processes to establish a design solution. The outcome should extend from the idea and not from the materials on hand. I am by no means an expert at this, yet I challenge myself with creating new work in this way.
